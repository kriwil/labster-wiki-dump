In a genome scan your aim is to identify DNA markers that are linked to an inherited trait in which the involved gene is unknown. By identifying the linked markers you narrow down the chromosomal region and can focus your search for the involved gene to a fraction of the genome.

In a genome scan you genotype DNA markers covering the entire genome and perform linkage analysis to identify DNA markers that co-segregate with the inherited trait.
